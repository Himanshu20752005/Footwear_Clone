        The Best Nike High-Top Sneakers You Can Buy Right Now

        Buying Guide

        From Air Force 1s to boot-like sneakers, explore the latest high-top shoe styles by Nike.

        Last updated: 29 November 2022
        3 min read

        Looking for a classic, high-top sneaker that covers the ankle and provides a sturdy, durable fit? If you love the look and feel of high-top sneakers, this Nike shoe round-up is for you. From timeless, retro styles to chunky kicks, these Nike high-top sneakers are ready to shop.

        Shop Nike High Tops

        View all

        1. Nike Air Force 1

        An iconic shoe that can be worn with almost anything, the Air Force 1 has been a defining style in sneaker culture since the 1980s. This shoe was originally designed for performance basketball, with Nike Air cushioning and a padded collar and tongue that create a plush, comfortable feel.

        The Air Force 1 has evolved to become a top-selling lifestyle shoe, available in a wide range of colours, materials and styles. Many mid- and high-top Air Force 1 styles feature an ankle strap with a hook-and-loop closure to customise the style and fit.

        (Related: The Best Nike Platform Sneakers)

        Shop Nike Air Force 1 High Tops

        View all

        2. Nike Dunk

        Similar to the Air Force 1, the Nike Dunk was originally released as a basketball shoe in the '80s. Thanks to the flat, grippy soles and creative styles and colourways, the shoe caught the eye of skaters. Today, both low- and high-top Dunks remain beloved.

        You'll find high-top styles with a mix of textures, like plush, varsity letterman-style Swoosh logos and leather uppers that are made to soften with age. Some high-top Dunks feature premium materials, like slub canvas underlays for added texture.

        Shop Nike Dunk High Tops

        View all

        3. Nike Blazer

        While Nike Blazers might be considered a mid-top shoe, they'll still cover the ankle and offer a timeless, vintage-inspired look. For example, the Nike Blazer Mid '77 Vintage features a crisp leather upper, exposed foam on the tongue and suede accents for a premium feel.

        You'll find other versions of the Blazer made in bold colourways and with corduroy textures and oversized Swoosh designs for a twist on the classic shoe.

        Shop Nike Blazers

        View all

        4. Nike Boots

        If you're looking for versatile high tops that can be styled with everyday outfits and double as durable, chilly-weather boots, there are plenty of styles to choose from. Nike All Conditions Gear (ACG) styles, for example, are well-suited for snowy roads and outdoor adventures, with weatherproof GORE-TEX technology in the upper.

        Air Force 1 boots are a notch more heavy-duty than a standard high-top Air Force 1. The Lunar Force 1, for example, features water-repellent leather in the upper, a rugged outsole and synthetic insulation to keep feet warm.

        For the toughest boots in the Nike collection, check out Nike Special Field Boots. Available in neutral colours and made from durable materials like leather and GORE-TEX, these boots generally feature an aggressive tread pattern and an internal rock shield to provide traction and protection over challenging terrain.

        (Related: The Toughest Tactical Boots to Buy From Nike)
